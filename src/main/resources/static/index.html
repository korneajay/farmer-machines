Our Mission

FormerMachines is a digital marketplace designed to address the challenges of farm mechanization in rural India. Our mission is to prevent crop losses during critical harvest seasons, eliminate machine supply deficits, and guarantee that every farmer has access to fair and affordable tractor and harvester rentals.

How It Works

  • Logistics Routing: Calculate travel delivery costs dynamically based on the shortest road path between villages.
  • Secure OTP Verification: Secure your booking tokens. The operator must verify a 4-digit OTP from the farmer upon arriving at the field.
  • Fleet Utilization: Help local equipment owners rent out idle machinery (tractors, rotavators, combine harvesters) to nearby farms.
